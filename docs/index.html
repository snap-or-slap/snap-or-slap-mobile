🚀 Mobile App Case Study

Rate products with a
Snap or Slap

A fun, swipe-based mobile app that lets users quickly review everyday products — snap to love it, slap to pass. Built with React Native & Firebase.

View Figma Prototype Read Behance Case Study
📱
The Problem

Product reviews are broken

Long-form reviews are hard to write and harder to trust. Young consumers want fast, visual, peer-driven ratings — not walls of text.

😩

Review fatigue

Only 5–10 % of buyers leave reviews. Writing paragraphs feels like homework.

🤷

Low trust

Fake reviews and sponsored posts make it hard to know what's genuine.

📵

No mobile-first flow

Most review platforms are desktop-first forms — clunky on a phone.

Why Snap or Slap?

One gesture. One honest opinion.

Snap or Slap distils product reviews into a single, satisfying swipe. No typing, no star-math — just your gut reaction.

⚡

Instant reviews

Swipe right to Snap (love it) or left to Slap (pass). A review in under two seconds.

🎯

Honest signal

Binary feedback removes rating inflation — no more guessing whether 3.7 stars is good.

🤳

Built for mobile

Native swipe gestures, camera-first scanning, and buttery-smooth animations.

MVP Scope

Core features, zero bloat

The minimum-viable product ships with just enough features to validate the core interaction — swipe reviews + social sharing.

👆

Swipe to review

Snap or Slap any product in seconds.

📸

Barcode scan

Point your camera to identify a product instantly.

📊

Snap ratio

See a live community ratio — no fake stars.

🔗

Share cards

Auto-generated visual cards for social media.

How It Works

Three taps, one review

1

Open & scan

Launch the app and scan a product barcode — or search by name.

2

Snap or Slap

Swipe right to Snap (recommend) or left to Slap (pass). Done!

3

Share & explore

See the live community ratio and share a visual card on social media.
