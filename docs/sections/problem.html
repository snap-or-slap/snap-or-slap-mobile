The Problem

Product reviews are broken

Long-form reviews are hard to write and harder to trust. Young consumers want fast, visual, peer-driven ratings — not walls of text.

😩

Review fatigue

Only 5–10 % of buyers leave reviews. Writing paragraphs feels like homework.

🤷

Low trust

Fake reviews and sponsored posts make it hard to know what's genuine.

📵

No mobile-first flow

Most review platforms are desktop-first forms — clunky on a phone.
